Maldives

Maldives is a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean. It is known for its stunning white sand beaches, crystal clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs. The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean, southwest of Sri Lanka and India.

Italy

Italy is a country in Southern Europe known for its rich history, art, culture, and cuisine. It is home to some of the world's most famous landmarks, including the Colosseum, the Leaning Tower of Pisa, and the Vatican City.

Japan

Japan is an island nation in East Asia known for its unique blend of traditional and modern culture. It is home to ancient temples, stunning natural landscapes, and bustling metropolises like Tokyo and Kyoto. Shibuya has the bussiest crossings in the world.
